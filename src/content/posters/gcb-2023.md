Single-nucleus RNA-seq of mouse (and human) adipocytes and immune cells across low- and high-fat diet conditions. We characterise lipid-associated macrophages (LAM) and fibro-inflammatory vs normal adipocyte states, quantify cell–cell interactions (CellChat), and show that animal weight correlates with the LAM/Adipo ratio — a proposed ordering axis for single-cell samples when batch effects are limited.

This poster presents work from my research thesis in the Adlung lab at UKE, carried out alongside human medicine studies and the Digital Health elective (2nd track) in Hamburg.
